## Storage-Network (StoNet) Research Group 👋
We are **Storage-Network (StoNet) Research Group** at VU Amsterdam, lead by [Animesh Trivedi](https://animeshtrivedi.github.io/). We design, build, evaluate, and optimize open-source systems. Here are a couple of useful links: 
  * Group's website: [https://stonet-research.github.io/](https://stonet-research.github.io/)
  * Our open-source repos (more are coming): [https://github.com/orgs/stonet-research/repositories](https://github.com/orgs/stonet-research/repositories)
  * Some useful notes that we have collected over the years: [https://stonet-research.github.io/wiki/wiki.html](https://stonet-research.github.io/wiki/wiki.html)
  * We are a diverse team of Bsc, Msc, and PhD students. Who is who (work-in-progress page): [https://stonet-research.github.io/people/people.html](https://stonet-research.github.io/people/people.html)
